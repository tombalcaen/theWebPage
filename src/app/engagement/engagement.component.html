Our engagement models

We are fully capable to satisfy your demands of higher flexibility by customizing any engagement model

fixed price

We offer fixed price - ficed scope models for projects where scope and requirements are reasonably clear. This low-risk model defines timelines and requested deliverables as mutually agreed upon. Because our methodology is Agile base, the fixed price will be determined by the story size analysed during the planning phase.

milestone billing

For the complex and long-lasting projects with clear phases we recommend a milestone approach. These milestones can be time-based or feature-based. As each milestone is accomplished, the project owner is expensed a pre-defined sum or portion of the whole project. Advantages of this model is the reduced risk associated with large-scale opperations.
