Test your product's hypothesis in weeks, not months

Our mvp lab is the perfect way for aspiring entrepreneurs to evaluate their new visions and market assumptions. Your Minimum Viable Product gives you the opportunity to really measure the market's mind and evaluate where opportunities lay.

We take entrepreneurs from idea to digital innovation

It starts with your business idea, your vision. Those Idea's tend to start small but have the potential to grow big. That's why it is important to see MVP-lab as a process not a fixed assignment. Let us guide you in our LEAN process. Designed for fast and transparent results.

Focus on core problem

The lean approach focusses on solving core problems with a minimal set of features. Our team makes sure those core features are user ready and measure their feedback.

Iterative process

The product owner is in full control of the process and the direction in which it goes. Your appointed dedicated team lead takes frequent communication very seriously to discuss integrated features and next steps.

Cost efficient

Starting with the mvp process let's you control and allocate your budget to where it truly matters. don't waste time and money on useless feature nobody really cares about.

Development Process

All our MVP projects pass four phases. Aiming to meterialize your vision and efficiently build your desired software. After every iteration of the four steps, the feedback can be analysed and the process will loop back and startover improving on the gathered information.

Discovery

What is your vision? Our target is to understand your idea and what problem you are trying to solve. Define the requirements and create a project scope.

Design

A wireframe or prototype of the requirements will be designed to give you an initial view and estimation of the potential solution. Communication will allow the project owner to iterate according to his vision.

Development

Our team of talented coders will build the core features based on the discovery and design process.

Delivery

The mvp will be delivered into the wild aimed at your target audience, ready to gather feedback and measure the marketmind.
